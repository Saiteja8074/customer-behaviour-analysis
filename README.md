## 📌 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate massive amounts of transactional data but often struggle to convert it into **actionable insights**.

The key challenge is:

> ❓ How can we analyze customer shopping behavior to improve **revenue, retention, and decision-making**?

### 🎯 Objectives
- Identify **high-value customers**
- Understand **purchase patterns & trends**
- Analyze **impact of discounts and subscriptions**
- Evaluate **product performance & customer satisfaction**
- Support **data-driven business strategies**

---

## 🧠 Solution Approach

This project simulates a **real-world data analyst workflow** by transforming raw retail data into meaningful insights using:

- 🐍 **Python** → Data Cleaning & Exploratory Analysis  
- 🗄️ **SQL** → Business Problem Solving  
- 📊 **Power BI** → Dashboard & Storytelling
